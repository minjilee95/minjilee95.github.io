Our Goal

Considering adoption? Not sure which animal will best suit your lifestyle? Pairbud will help you to find the perfect pet depending on your current situation.

6.5 million companion animals enter animal shelters every year.
Our goal is to give these animals a home quicker and with a higher success rate of both you and the animal adapting to the change.

Pairbud aims to solve many common adoption roadblocks by providing the following services:
• Our interactive friend finder and map allow you to find nearby pets of multiple species
• Our specialized search criteria matches you with animals who'll fit well in your environment
• All listed pets provide their information, as well as shelter contact info
• It's easy to find different kinds of pets, all in one place!
